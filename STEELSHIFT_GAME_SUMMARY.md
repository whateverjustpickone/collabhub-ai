## üéÆ GAME OVERVIEW

Steel Shift is an **industrial factory simulation / tycoon game** focusing on the steel manufacturing and construction industries. Players manage the complete supply chain from foundry to construction site.

### Genre
- Factory Simulation
- Business Tycoon
- Management Strategy

### Target Platforms
- **PC**: Steam, Epic Games Store
- **Mobile**: iOS, Android
- **Pricing**: $30 base game (PC), freemium model (mobile)

---

## üè≠ THE 7-MODULE ECOSYSTEM

Steel Shift consists of 7 interconnected gameplay modules:

### 1. **Mill Works** (Foundry Simulation)
- Molten steel processing
- Ladle operations
- Slab casting
- Raw material production

### 2. **Angle of Attack** (Fabrication)
- CNC cutting, punching, drilling
- Precision grinding
- Material stacking and sorting
- Equipment: Peddinghaus drills, Messer cutting systems

### 3. **Loader's Lament** (Logistics)
- Forklift operations
- Warehouse management
- Material staging and dispatch
- Inventory optimization

### 4. **Beam Me Up** (Cranes & Rigging)
- Gantry crane operations
- Overhead crane management
- Heavy load rigging
- Safety protocols

### 5. **Fitter's Folly** (Welding & QA)
- Fit-up operations
- Welding simulation
- Quality assurance checks
- Safety inspections

### 6. **Skyline Crew** (Construction)
- On-site assembly
- Tower crane operations
- Mobile crane management
- Skyline construction sequences

### 7. **SteelShift Tycoon** (Management Hub)
- Holographic control center
- Financial management
- Resource allocation
- Strategic planning
- KPI tracking

### In-Game Sponsorship (INNOVATIVE)

#### Standard Sponsorship Tiers
| Tier | Features | Pricing |
|------|----------|---------|
| **Starter** | Branded signage/banners, monthly rotation | $2,000-$5,000/month |
| **Standard** | Branded equipment, scenario sponsorship, analytics | $7,000-$30,000/campaign |
| **Premium** | Full campaign, expansion packs, influencer deals, exclusive events | $30,000-$100,000+ |

#### Deep Interactive Sponsorships (UNIQUE INNOVATION)
**What Makes Steel Shift Different:**

1. **Fully Operable Branded Equipment**
   - Example: Peddinghaus BDL 1250/9 drill with realistic controls
   - Authentic UI, displays, and performance metrics
   - Advanced simulation for serious players

2. **Training Integration**
   - Direct links to manufacturer training portals
   - Performance comparison: "With the real Peddinghaus drill, your output would have been 18% higher"
   - Certificate/credential earning opportunities

3. **Opt-In Model**
   - Basic players: Play without ads/distractions
   - Advanced/Pro players: Unlock realistic branded equipment
   - Preserves immersion for all audiences

4. **Performance-Driven Sales**
   - Contextual sales pitches based on in-game metrics
   - "Visit Peddinghaus.com to see full specs"
   - Lead generation for B2B industrial sponsors

5. **Regional Customization**
   - Dynamic sponsor swapping by user region
   - US: Peddinghaus, Caterpillar
   - UK/India: Tata Steel
   - Germany: Messer Cutting Systems
